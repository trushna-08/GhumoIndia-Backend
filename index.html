Current Festivals in India

Diwali

The Festival of Lights symbolizes the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil. Celebrated with beautiful diyas, lanterns, and fireworks, homes are decorated with rangoli and families exchange gifts and sweets.

November 12, 2024 Pan India

Holi

The Festival of Colors welcomes spring with vibrant celebrations. People gather to play with colors, share traditional sweets, and enjoy folk music. The festival promotes unity, equality, and joyful community bonding.

March25, 2024 Pan India

Navratri

Nine nights dedicated to Goddess Durga, featuring traditional dance forms like Garba and Dandiya. Each night celebrates different forms of the goddess with music, dance, and spiritual rituals.

October 3-11, 2024 Pan India
